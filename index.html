About Us

Groupder is an app that allows you to follow calenders. Follow your friends or family or what you are passionate about to stay up to date, find cool events, or find great times to meet up.

Features

Follow what events your friends or family are planning.
Customize your own feed to follow the events that you care about. Stay up to date with what you care about.
Create your own events and invite your friends or family. Or create events that anyone can attend.
